Introduction

Currently, computer vision and deep learning are growing strongly and this will be a potential area for research and development. The application fields of computer vision are very rich, which can be mentioned as defect detection, facial recognition, anatomy, robotics, security systems,... In which, image recognition The human situation is also one of the problems in the field that new technologies approach to solve in an intelligent way. Most families today install security cameras in the home, taking advantage of that, we can monitor human activities through the camera. Suppose a family member has a stroke and faints, but the camera is not always monitored by family members, which could lead to the fainted person not being detected by family members. taken to the hospital, the worst case can adversely affect life. In this paper, a skeleton-based approach to human posture recognition is proposed to identify the fall posture of people to help warn when a fall action is detected to help ensure safety for people. that can develop into a smart virtual assistant in the home.

Method

Build and train LSTM model to recognize human fall action.

Duty

Dataset

This dataset contain 24 scenarios recorded with 8 IP video cameras. The first 22 first scenarios contain a fall and confounding events, the last 2 ones contain only confounding events.
